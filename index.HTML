    Marching Band and the Director

    Marching Band is such a fun sport. We have a lot of fun playing music and listening to our show music and finding out what our show is going to be for that season. Band is A LOT of hard work but it's worth it in the end. We have amazing band directors. Meg Beavers is our main director. She's very funny, kind, and makes every season a great one. Doug Perry and Pam Waits are the couches for the Color Guard. Both of them are very witty and help the guard to be the best that they can be. Mr. Perry is so amazing at coming up with work for our show. Shane Mathews is the director for the percussion/battery. Mr.Mathews is funny and he helps Ms.Beavers with the band too. Our band directors make a great team to make our band is the best and making each season one to remember.

    Color Guard

    I love doing Color Guard for the Licking Heights High School. It's such an amazing experience. I love getting to meet new people every year. Being in guard is so amazing. The costumes the guard wears are really unique and go great with our shows theme. The flags we use for our show are so beautiful. Guard is the best section of the band. We get everyone excited for football games and we get along with everyone in the band. Even though is looks hard, its actually not. Once you get the hang of it, you'll look like a pro. I love the group of people I'm with. I wouldn't trade it for anything. This is going to be my third year in guard and I can't wait to see how this year will turn out. We're a huge family and we enjoy being around each other.

    What we do in Band

    Towards the end of July and early August we have band camp.It's from 8am-4pm everyday for two weeks. We have Saturday and Sunday off. During band camp we learn our show that we will perform. We have practice Monday-Friday 3-5:15 after school. It's a lot of hard work but it's worth it. Friday nights we have football games and they are the best. We all have a blast playing music, acting crazy, and watching the football players win. At half-time we perform our show for the school. Then on Saturdays we have competitions. Sometimes we have two competitions in a day. Winning a competition is so much fun. The whole band gets excited when the judges annouce that we're going to state finals. Band is such a great experience and you'll have so much fun.

    Our Band Parents

    Our band has amazing band parents. They support everyone of us. Our parents always make sure that we have water when we have parades, make sure all of our instruments and equipment are loaded to go for every football game and competition. Our parents also help with the music boosters. They all give a helping hand to raise money so the band can get new uniforms, new music, new props for the show, and so much more. Our parents want to see everyone happy, healthy, and most of all they make sure that we have food. Band kids love food so, the parents always make sure that all the band students are fed and ready to go. We love all of our band parents and we appreciate them all for their hard work and their support of this band.
